Our Story

Passionate about Africa since 2008

African Trails was born from a deep love for the continent's breathtaking landscapes, rich cultures, and incredible wildlife.

Founded in 2008 by wildlife photographer James Mwangi and conservation expert Sarah Omondi, our journey began with a simple mission: to share the authentic beauty of Africa while preserving it for generations to come.

What started as a small operation with just three guides has grown into a respected travel company with over 50 dedicated team members across East and Southern Africa. Despite our growth, we've maintained our core values of authenticity, sustainability, and exceptional personalized service.

James Mwangi

Founder & CEO

Our Mission

To create transformative African travel experiences that connect people with the continent's natural wonders and cultural heritage while supporting conservation and community development.

  • Deliver exceptional, authentic experiences
  • Promote sustainable tourism practices
  • Support wildlife conservation efforts
  • Empower local communities

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable travel company in Africa, recognized for our commitment to conservation, community empowerment, and creating life-changing travel experiences.

  • Set the standard for responsible tourism
  • Expand our conservation impact
  • Innovate in sustainable travel solutions
  • Inspire global appreciation for Africa
